1. Description of Service

SpeakEasy Invoice is a mobile application for iOS and Android designed for tradespeople in Ireland. The App provides:

  • Voice-first invoicing: Record job details through a guided voice workflow. Your recordings are transcribed using OpenAI Whisper and parsed with GPT-4o to generate professional PDF invoices on your device.
  • Receipt and expense tracking: Capture receipts via your camera. Optical character recognition (OCR) powered by Google Gemini extracts expense details automatically.
  • Google Drive integration: Optionally store generated invoices and receipt images in your personal Google Drive account.
  • Google Sheets integration: Optionally log job and expense records to spreadsheets in your Google account.
  • On-device data storage: All job data, settings, and business information are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a backend database or store your data on our servers.
